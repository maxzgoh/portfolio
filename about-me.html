About Me.

Max Goh.

Product Designer based in Los Angeles.

Profile.

I am a passionate, creative, and detailed-oriented product designer, who strives to solve user problems with intentional and elegant solutions. With the end user in mind, I approach challenges with empathy, enthusiasm, and purpose while meeting business goals.

Story.

Previously, I was in advertising operations and account management at Snap Inc. (Snapchat), managing national digital campaigns for the consumer vertical. My main clients included Google, Amazon, Sony Interactive Media, Ubisoft, T-Mobile, and Gap.

I identified ineffiencies with our process for campaign setup. So, I took the initiative of designing and building a task management system for the global ad operations and account management teams. As a result, there was a significant increase in operational efficiency and decrease in campaign trafficking errors, which saved time, minimized makegoods, and alleviated pain points for my colleagues. I then decided to change my career to product design as I truly enjoyed creating a useful product for users.

Now, my main focus is to continue to make an impact for users and businesses as a product designer.
